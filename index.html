Info

 Time

Due to climatic conditions, we can safely fly only in the early morning hours with the primary season and the beautiful months of May through November. Since our launch times varies through the seasons, we ask our guests to call ahead for the exact meeting time.

 Weather

High winds and/or inclement weather prevent us from flying. We ask all our guests to contact us in the early afternoon prior to their scheduled flight between 12:00 noon and 2:00 p.m. to verify with the forecasted weather conditions will allow for the balloon flight.

 Flight

Our actual flight time is approximately one to one and a half hours averaging closer to one and a half hours and we suggest to allow three to three and a half hours for the entire adventure.

 Participation

We encourage our guests, if they wish, to participate in both the inflation and deflation of the balloon. We have found to adds greatly for the fun of the entire ballooning experience
